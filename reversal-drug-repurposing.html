Methodology

Four steps, each independently checkable

Every candidate that reaches you has passed through a pipeline where a failure at any step surfaces immediately, instead of hiding inside a single confident-sounding answer.

01 — PROFILE
Phenotype & signature
Symptoms and gene-expression signature of the new disease are captured as structured input.
02 — VECTORIZE
Convert to vector
The signature becomes a numeric vector across shared marker genes, comparable by cosine similarity.
03 — MATCH
Search known diseases
The vector is compared against reference diseases to find the closest biological footprint.
04 — VALIDATE
Rank by reversal
Approved drugs for the matched disease are ranked by how strongly they push the signature in reverse.
